About

Outreach 2021

Society for Space Education, Research and Development (SSERD) is an independent, non-profit organization dedicated to making space education accessible to everyone globally. We have reached 50,000+ students and over 20,000 students from more than 50+ countries through various space programs during the pandemic.

SSERD's outreach program, in a true sense, is an example of connecting space enthusiasts throughout the world, giving them a chance to acknowledge the beauty of space, science, and technology, thereby unraveling some of the mysterious questions that still lie out there.

So this year, grab the opportunity to be a part of 1 Million space enthusiasts, including school students, undergraduates, teachers, and the common public globally, through our ten days free online space courses, which will run along with the World Space Week celebration.

What is the aim of this program?

SSERD Space Outreach Program aims to reach out to 1M+ people through our ten days free online space course. This program will give students a strong basics of Space Science and Technology, branches of Space Technology, Career Opportunities, etc. This initiative is a robust approach made by the SSERD Team to reach out to many students.
