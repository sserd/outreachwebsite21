Imaginative Space designs

  • Target audience : Undergraduates
  • No. of participants: Individual / group upto 5 members
  • Details:

    With great enthusiasm, we present to you an event filled with zest, ‘Interstellar Design Competition’. I.D.C. is an opportunity to discover your imagination along with your architectural skills, creativity and much more.

    Imagine this unique scenario. Humans, having destroyed most resources available naturally, the global climate conditions lead us to a herculean task of exploring other worlds. And to do so, we require spaceships, designs of colonies etc. Execution of this duty requires the best of designers to gear up and put on their thinking caps! So all you need to do is make designs on the ideas you think will help in continuing our species' existence and submit them. Format of submission: Upload the document in Google Slides with a basic description of your idea.

  • Deadline: To submit your completed work (13 th october 2021) Winners: Certificates to top 3 participants + surprise prize.

Ideator’s Extravaganza!

  • Target audience: Undergraduates
  • No. of participants: Individual/group up to 5 members
  • Details: A problem statement related to the current challenge in space will be provided to you on 29th August 2021, which will test your logical and organized skills. All you need to do is give the most efficient solution
  • Format of submission: A Google Doc has to be created with a clear description of the idea
  • Deadline:To submit your completed work (12th October 2021)
  • Winners: Certificates to top 3 participants + surprise prize
  • Judging criteria: Special Judges
  • Problem Statement:

    • Humanity, with its current technological capabilities takes about 9 months for a trip to planet Mars. The travel-time stands as a major hurdle in achieving the long-standing ambition to colonize Mars. That is where we require teams of sharp minds to brainstorm and develop an idea, a key to unlock the door to our civilization's future.

    'Find an efficient way to reduce the time for a trip to Mars.'

    Bear in mind, long voyages take a toll on the human body.

    Note: Solutions covering such additional challenges will be awarded points while judging.
