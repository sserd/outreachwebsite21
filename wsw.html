Women In Space

This year's theme is WOMEN IN SPACE, honouring the extraordinary journey of talented, intelligent women in the space industry. It gives immense pleasure to say that SSERD is the vision that 23-year-old entrepreneur Nikhitha C. had when she co-founded the successful Society for Space Education Research and Development (SSERD), based out in Bengaluru. At SSERD, we have always focused on giving women a solid platform to come forward and present themselves through our various past events. The entire week will be dedicated to celebrating the awards and achievements of exceptional women and their stunning contributions to their targeted fields. We also believe in sharing knowledge and growing together as a community. We will be emphasized by organizing various sessions, discussions and training for our fellow sisters and mates who can benefit from it and rise in all aspects, including the career, personality, and whole as an individual.
